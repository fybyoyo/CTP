Unlike many other wildgame processors, CTP does not batch process your meat. Batch processing means your meat and everyone elses meat gets mixed together and you are given back your weight portion. This means that if another hunter does not properly care for his animal, then you may end up with some of that meat. When you bring us an animal for processing, it is tracked and segregated from the time it enters the facility until you come pick up the package product. You can rest assured that what you brought us you are getting only that back.
